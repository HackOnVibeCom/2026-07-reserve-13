# HackOnVibe — Project Questionnaire

**1. What does your application/service do?**

Tact drafts venue-native "soft" community posts for a newly launched product — without the hard sell — for Reddit, Indie Hackers, Product Hunt maker notes, and X. It also shows the spam version you almost wrote, scores spam posture with a deterministic rules engine, lets you edit and re-score, and share a judge link.

**2. Who is the target audience?**

Solo builders in the first 0–30 days after shipping a product. Target rooms include r/SideProject, r/iOSProgramming, Indie Hackers, r/androiddev, Product Hunt maker, and X/Twitter.

**5. What is your advantage?**

Soft-vs-spam dual drafts backed by a deterministic spam-risk engine with before/after meters, plus edit-soft-draft → re-score. Demo drafts work with no API key (live drafts are optional via OpenRouter), and the free tier gives 3 live drafts per browser with unlimited demo drafts.
